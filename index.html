About

About Iberovita

Iberovita is a Spain-focused beverage distribution initiative currently in its launch phase. It is being developed with the objective of identifying and introducing differentiated, health-oriented European beverage brands into selected channels across the Spanish market.

The project is built around a clear commercial rationale: consumer demand for functional, convenient and better-positioned beverage options continues to grow, while many high-potential European brands remain underrepresented in Spain.

Iberovita's approach is selective and long-term oriented, with a focus on building strong brand relationships and developing sustainable routes to market rather than pursuing purely transactional sales.

Market

Market Focus

Iberovita focuses on beverage concepts that combine quality, convenience and clear consumer positioning. The interest is in products that respond to the growing demand for better-for-you alternatives, including functional and health-oriented categories with potential for repeat consumption and broad market relevance.

The initial focus is on brands with strong product fundamentals, attractive positioning and realistic potential for channel-based market entry in Spain.

Quality product selection and disciplined market entry are at the core of our approach.

How we work

Partnership Approach

We are interested in working with European beverage brands that see Spain as a relevant growth market and value a committed local distribution partner with a disciplined go-to-market approach.

Our preferred model is based on selective brand partnerships, clear commercial alignment and a step-by-step market development logic. This means starting with the right product and channel combinations, generating market feedback and expanding with structure.

Iberovita is particularly interested in brands that combine strong product quality with clear positioning and are looking to build sustainable presence in Spain over time.
